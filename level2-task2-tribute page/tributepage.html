Emma Watson

About

Emma Watson is a British actress, model, and activist. She rose to prominence playing Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series, and since then, she has become an influential figure in the entertainment industry and beyond.

Aside from her acting career, Emma is known for her advocacy work, particularly in promoting gender equality and women's rights. She has served as a UN Women Goodwill Ambassador and launched the HeForShe campaign, which calls for men to advocate for gender equality.

Emma continues to inspire millions around the world through her talent, intelligence, and dedication to making a positive impact.

"I don't want other people to decide who I am. I want to decide that for myself." - Emma Watson
